Nutrition Tool.

Set your calories and macros from your own numbers, tell it the foods you like and the ones you don't, then build a week of meals from 200 recipes. Choose bulk-prep or cook-fresh for each meal, breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks. Bulk meals only use recipes that keep and reheat well. Everything runs in your browser, nothing is sent anywhere.

1. Your numbers

Used to estimate your daily energy and macro targets. Rough is fine, you can override the target below.
On the deficit and surplus: these are kept in a sustainable range on purpose. Cutting much harder than this under-fuels your training and recovery; a bigger surplus is mostly fat, not muscle. A calorie floor also stops the target dropping too low. Slower and steady wins here.

2. Your daily target

Protein set from bodyweight, fats at 25% of calories, carbs fill the rest. Edit any value and the plan will aim at it.

3. Build the plan

Pick how many meals a day and how you want to eat, then make it yours with likes, dislikes, and filters.
2 (IF) = two meals, intermittent-fasting style, in a shorter eating window.

Your week
