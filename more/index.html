About

True Hybrid is a complete framework for building a hybrid athlete across three layers: physical capacity, mental command, and spiritual direction. The framework is the master text; the tools are the free, working front door to it.

Almost none of the parts were invented from nothing. The progression principles, the oxygen-led view of the engine, the conditioning lineage, and the sports-psychology sources are named where they appear. What's ours is the way it's assembled, tested over years with hundreds of real people.
